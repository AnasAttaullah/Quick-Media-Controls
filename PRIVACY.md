## Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** March 29, 2026

`Quick Media Controls` respects user privacy. This app is designed to run locally on the device and does not require account sign-in.

### Data Collection
- No personal data is collected, sold, or shared.
- No telemetry, analytics, or advertising SDKs are used.

### Local Data Storage
- App settings (such as preferences and hotkeys) are stored locally on the device.

### Network Use
- Internet access is used only for app functionality, such as:
  - Checking for updates (when enabled)
  - Opening links that the user explicitly chooses

### Media Session Access
- The app reads currently playing media session information on the device (for example, track title/artist) only to provide media controls.
- This information is not transmitted to the developer.

### Permissions and Privileges
- The app runs at normal user level and does not request administrator elevation.

### Third-Party Services
- The app does not use third-party services to collect personal information.

### Changes to This Policy
- This privacy policy may be updated. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
